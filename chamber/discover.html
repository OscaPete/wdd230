Jubilife City- A Brief Summary

Jubilife City is one of the largest cities in the entire Sinnoh Region, and holds the epithet "The City of Joy". The city is located on the West side of the region, which is divided by the mighty Mt. Coronet, and sits between Floaroma Town to the North and Sandgem Town to the South.

Jubilife is home to many wonderful services, including but not limited to the Trainer's School, the Jubilife TV Station, and the Poketch Company. Many of these services accomodate the entirety of Sinnoh, making Jubilife a hotspot for tourists due to the exclusive nature of these locations. Why not try visiting some of these spots yourself?

In its past, Jubilife City was once called Jubilife Village, and was the original major settlement of Sinnoh. Its inhabitants worked hard on documenting the wildlife of the region so that our present day residents could rest easy, knowing all they need to know about the wildlife.
