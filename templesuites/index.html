Spotlight Summary: The Salt Lake City Temple

The SLC temple is easily the most iconic temple out there, sitting smack dab in the middle of the city, surrounded by the ever beautiful Temple Square, Conference Center, and other buildings and services owned by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. This temple was a mighty undertaking to build, taking a whopping 40 years to complete. Currently, it is under renovations to restore this beautiful temple, and for the first time in a very very long time, it will be open to the public during an open house when renovations complete.
