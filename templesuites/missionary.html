Temple Missionary Suites

We are proud to offer special, long-term residency suites to temple missionaries who need housing. These suites are quite obviously equipped for long-term residency, and are built to be essentially the same as an apartment room. Each suite has a master bedroom, two bathrooms, a combination kitchen and living room, laundry room, and an additional room that can be used either as a second bedroom, or a study room. Or anything else, really! Each suite is fully furnished, and if you stay in one that isn't, let us know! We will happily fix the issue as quickly as possible.
